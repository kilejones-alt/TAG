Anti-Zionism / Antizionism

This topic is now separated into three independent entries.

  • Anti-Zionism, 1880–1947Early religious, socialist, liberal, and assimilationist objections to Zionism before Israel existed.
  • Anti-Zionism, 1948–October 6, 2023How opposition to Jewish statehood changed after Israel’s founding and before October 7.
