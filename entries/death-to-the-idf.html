Graffiti slogan / soldier-targeting chant / elimination wish

Death to the IDF

The phrase turns hatred of Israel’s army into a public wish for dead Jewish soldiers and those serving beside them.

This placeholder will cover “Death to the IDF” as a slogan aimed at the army of the world’s only Jewish-majority state. The entry should note that the phrase is not a policy objection; it celebrates dead soldiers, many of them conscripts, reservists, medics, Druze, Bedouin, immigrants, parents, and young adults.
